Our Mission & Values

At Adum Central Church of Christ, we are devoted to restoring New Testament practices. We commit ourselves to proclaiming God's Word, nurturing a community united in biblical truth, and serving our neighbors with humility and grace.

Scripture

We believe in the sole authority of the Bible. Every sermon and study is grounded in a careful, expository teaching of God's Word.

Community

Our congregation is a family rooted in mutual support, accountability, and the love of Christ—a true fellowship that honors the New Testament model.

Service & Discipleship

We strive to live out our faith by serving one another, equipping believers through discipleship, and engaging in acts of kindness that reflect Christ’s love.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Church of Christ - Kumasi Central has faithfully served our community for over 70 years. Rooted in the principles of the New Testament, our church is dedicated to proclaiming the Gospel and nurturing discipleship.

We remain committed to a simple yet profound worship experience—centered on Scripture, prayer, and authentic fellowship.

Our Core Values

Biblical principles that guide our worship, fellowship, and service.

Faith

We anchor our lives in the unwavering truth of God's Word and strive to reflect that faith in every aspect of our service.

Community

We are a family of believers committed to genuine fellowship, mutual support, and shared accountability as modeled by the early church.

Service

Our service is a humble, Bible-centered gathering, where we worship with simplicity, study the Scriptures, and serve one another in love.
