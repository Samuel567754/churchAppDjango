What to Expect

Your first visit to Adum Central Church of Christ

Worship Experience

At Adum Central Church of Christ, our worship is marked by simplicity and a deep reverence for Scripture. Experience pure congregational worship with a cappella singing, earnest prayer, and expository preaching that reflects the practices of the early church.

Worship Style

Our services are unaccompanied by instruments, embracing a cappella singing that honors the historic simplicity of biblical worship.

Preaching & Teaching

Expect thoughtful, expository preaching that unpacks the Scriptures, delivering clear, Bible-based teaching to guide your spiritual growth.

Fellowship

Connect with fellow believers in genuine, heartfelt fellowship after the service—a time for prayer, conversation, and building lasting relationships.
